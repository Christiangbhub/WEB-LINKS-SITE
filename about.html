ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Christian George — a web developer, graphic designer, and Computer Programming student based in North York, Toronto. I enjoy building clean, functional digital experiences and visually appealing designs. Whether it’s a website, a flyer, a poster, or a small full-stack app, I love creating things that look good and work smoothly.

I’m currently in my second year, third semester of my Computer Programming diploma, where I’ve been developing strong skills in Java, JavaScript, Angular, React/Next.js, SQL, Git, and building real applications like REST APIs with Spring Boot. On the design side, I use Photoshop and Canva to create professional graphics and layouts for practice, personal projects, and clients. Outside school, I spend time building projects on GitHub, improving my design portfolio on Behance, and learning new technologies that help me grow as a developer and designer. I like combining both worlds — code and design — to create simple, modern, and user-friendly work.
